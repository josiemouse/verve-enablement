How to use Sales Enablement
💬
Ask AI
Ask natural language questions to get material recommendations for any partner situation, use case or format need.
Ask by partner type — "what should I send a retargeting DSP?"
Ask by format or product — "what covers CTV for brand buyers?"
When materials are recommended, click ↗ Open to open directly, or + Pack to save them
The conversation keeps context — you can follow up or refine
📚
Library
Browse and search the full catalogue. Filter by platform and audience to narrow down quickly.
Use the search bar to filter by keyword across titles, platforms and types
Filter by Platform and Audience to narrow down
Click ↗ to open, 🔗 to copy the link, + to add to your pack
🕐
Recently Viewed
Materials you open are tracked in a strip at the top of the Library — useful for revisiting things before or during a call.
Tracks up to 6 materials per session
Resets when you close the browser tab
📦
Build a Pack
Curate a set of materials for a specific partner or meeting, then send them in one go.
Add via the + button in Library or + Pack from AI recommendations
The Pack button turns orange when you have items
Copy all links copies a plain list; Send via email opens your mail client pre-written
🔗
Copy Link
The 🔗 button on every card copies the URL straight to clipboard — no need to open the doc first.
A confirmation toast appears at the bottom of the screen when copied
